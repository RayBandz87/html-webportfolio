The Best Movies According to Raymond

My top 3 movies of all-time.

Django: Unchained

The movie is occasionally great but generally irritative. In the same masturbatory way that most Tarantino movies are good, this one is good.

Harlem Nights

"Harlem Nights" is a nice, enjoyable movie where Eddie Murphy doesn't overdo his street-smart shtick, where Pryor is the nice guy and occasionally a touching father figure who tries to keep things under control, and where Redd Foxx is the subject of a great running-gag involving his poor sight, his interactions with Della Reese are as enjoyable as Murphy's.

Walk Hard: Dewy Cox Story

Many tiny laughs are produced by the severe silliness that dominates most of Cox's life, but not enough memorable major moments are achieved.
